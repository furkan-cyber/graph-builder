# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Graph Builder is an engineering-focused open source project. Participation should stay respectful, direct, and constructive.

Expected behavior:
- Assume good intent and discuss technical tradeoffs in good faith.
- Give actionable review feedback instead of vague dismissal.
- Keep discussions focused on the code, behavior, documentation, and user impact.
- Respect different experience levels and communication styles.

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or exclusionary language.
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of issues, discussions, or pull requests.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, lock, or close content that makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.

## Reporting

If you encounter behavior that violates this code, contact the maintainers through the repository's available contact channels.
